Talks

In 2025, I gave two talks at the Homological Algebra Reading Group, coordinated by Will Hobkirk and Damian Lin. The first was an introductory talk on simplicial sets. The second talk introduced the Ext and Tor functors from the perspective of projective and injective resolutions, and presented a quick proof of the Universal Coefficient Theorem based on the exposition in Weibel's textbook. The latter talk was recorded and may be viewed here.
