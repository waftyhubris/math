Stable Homotopy Theory Reading Group

Week 1: Spectral Sequences

The first talk in our reading group! We will define spectral sequences, and use as our motivating example the spectral sequence of a filtered complex. We will then introduce the spectral sequence of a double complex, and produce quick and marvelous proofs of some results in homological algebra, such as the five lemma and the snake lemma.

Speaker: Declan Zammit, Macquarie University
